Madan Mohan Malaviya University of Technology

Madan Mohan Malaviya University of Technology, Gorakhpur has been established in year 2013 by the
Government of Uttar Pradesh in the form of a non-affiliating, teaching and research University after
reconstituting the Madan Mohan Malaviya Engineering College, Gorakhpur which was established in 1962.
